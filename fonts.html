Heroes Font Family

This industrial style sans serif typeface means business. Inpired by the likes of "Knockout!" and Swedish type, this is meant to add urgency to your message. Designed during the Black Lives Matter protests and Covid-19 pandemic of 2020, it was designed as a tool to help spread a message of hope and solidarity for those disenfranchised.
